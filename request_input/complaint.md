# Customer Complaints Guide

Customer complaints are expressions of dissatisfaction regarding a product, service, or customer experience. Common examples include defective products, unexpected hidden fees, delayed shipping, and rude service. Addressing these issues quickly prevents customer churn and helps businesses improve.

---

## 📋 Common Categories & Examples

### 1. Product or Quality Issues
* **The Complaint:** "The blender I bought stopped working after just two uses, despite following the manual exactly."
* **The Complaint:** "The jacket I received is a completely different shade of blue than the one shown in your online catalog."

### 2. Billing & Pricing Errors
* **The Complaint:** "I was charged €45 for a subscription I cancelled last month."
* **The Complaint:** "There are hidden delivery fees applied at checkout that were not mentioned on the item's page."

### 3. Service Delays & Delivery
* **The Complaint:** "My package was promised to arrive in 2 days, but it has been stuck in transit for over a week."
* **The Complaint:** "I was told a technician would arrive between 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM, but no one showed up or called."

### 4. Poor Customer Service
* **The Complaint:** "I was on hold for 45 minutes, and when I finally spoke to an agent, they were rude and couldn't help me."
* **The Complaint:** "Every time I call, I have to repeat my account details and get a different answer from each agent."

### 5. Website or App Issues
* **The Complaint:** "Your mobile app keeps crashing every time I try to complete my payment."
* **The Complaint:** "The promo code I received via email is returning an invalid error on your website."
